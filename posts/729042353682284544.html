Well, an ecollar dog owner said it’s not possible to make a dog walk backward 20 feet (6 feet) with positive reinforcement, even with treats.

Well, accomplished without treats and only a bumper.

Didn’t have a place board, so we used a branch and taught her to stand, sit and lay down behind the branch if she wants me to toss the toy.

Not only that, but 20 meters (65 feet) and not 20 feet.

To be fair, we cheated a bit since we already built a solid foundation with the fetch game. Once she was taught how to be sent on a retrieve, then teaching her to walk backward was easy-peasy.

Swear people are not very creative when it comes to training dogs.
