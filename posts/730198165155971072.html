Verdict on Purina ONE:

Zarya does not like the kibbles very much. That’s okay. Didn’t want to buy another bag of the Pro Plan while transitioning her to the Inukshuk 30/25 (arriving on October 10th).

She eats the nuggets first in the morning then eats the rest of the kibbles in the evening. She will gulp the whole bowl down but only at the end of the day.

CW: coprophagy 

She started engaging in coprophagy again. Not really a surprise since adding margarine to her meals halted that behaviour in the past when we used sample packs while the stores were closed. Purina ONE is about 11% lower in fat compared to the Pro Plan, so there’s that.

Will have to call the company for grams per 100 kCal for a true measurement of the kibble composition since the information on dog food packagings are generally useless except as legal documents.

The breeder was from Romania and she was fed a diet of raw meat and brown rice with margarine, so she was always a picky eater. So, don’t take the post as anything serious.

Trying to convert her to kibbles when we first got her was a real headache.

We only went with Purina Pro Plan because Canadian pet food regulations are loose compared to FDA and USDA in the U.S., and AAFCO isn’t as tight as FEDIAF. If we were in the EU, then we would be a lot more comfortable with a wider range of commercially-available dog food selections.
